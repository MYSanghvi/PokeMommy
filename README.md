# PokeMommy - Gotta Train ’Em All

Train like a Pokémon Master - even if you’re just starting your journey.

**PokeMommy** is a playful, full-featured Pokémon quiz app designed to help you master all Pokémon.

Originally created for a very specific Trainer… but built for anyone ready to level up their Pokédex knowledge.

---

## Why This Exists

Every Trainer starts somewhere.

This project began as a way to train my expecting wife - so that when our future little Pokémon Trainer arrives, this house is already operating at full Pokédex capacity.

No pressure, wifey.

---

## How to Play

1. Enter your Trainer name  
2. Choose a quiz mode  
3. Select difficulty (Easy or Hard)  
4. Test your Pokémon knowledge  
5. Climb the leaderboard and become a Pokémon Master  

---

## Game Modes

### Pokédex  
Browse all the Pokémon.  
View their type, category, Pokédex entry, and full evolution line.  
Tap the speaker to hear the name pronounced.

### Identify the Pokémon  
A Pokémon’s name is shown. Choose the correct image from four options.  
**Hard mode:** All images appear as silhouettes.

### Spot the Evolution  
A Pokémon is shown. Choose what it evolves into - or what it evolved from.  
Be careful… some Pokémon don’t evolve.  
**Hard mode:** Sprites appear as silhouettes.

### Who’s That Pokémon?  
A Pokémon sprite is shown. Choose the correct name from four options.  
**Hard mode:** The sprite is a silhouette.

### Gym Battles  
Face themed quizzes inspired by Gym Leaders.  
Each battle tests your knowledge of specific Pokémon types - just like in the original games.  
Win your battles, earn your badges, and prove you’re ready for the Pokémon League.  
**Hard mode:** The sprite is a silhouette.

---

## Features

- **Quick Test** - 20 random Pokémon per session  
- **Full Pokédex Mode** - All Pokémon in one run  
- **Easy / Hard Difficulty** - Clear images vs. silhouettes  
- **3-Tier Hint System** - Category → Type → Pokédex entry  
- **Built-in Pronunciation** - Hear how each Pokémon name is spoken  
- **Auto-Advance** - Next question loads automatically  
- **Leaderboard** - Ranked by accuracy and time  
- **Sound & Vibration Feedback** - For correct answers, wrong answers, hints, and results  
- **Mobile-Friendly** - Swipe navigation for Pokédex browsing
